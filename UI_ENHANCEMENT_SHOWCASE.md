## STYLING IMPROVEMENTS SUMMARY

### **Typography**
- Font: Segoe UI, Arial (Professional)
- Sizes: 11-28px (Proper hierarchy)
- Weights: Bold for headers and important text
- Colors: White, dark blue, custom colors

### **Spacing**
- Padding: 15-25px (Comfortable)
- Margins: 10-15px (Breathing room)
- Gaps: 10-15px between buttons
- Line height: Proper readability

### **Borders & Shadows**
- Radius: 3-8px (Modern rounded)
- Width: 1px (Subtle)
- Colors: Matching section colors
- Effects: Professional appearance

### **Responsive Design**
- Buttons: 100-150px width (Clickable)
- Tables: Full width with proper columns
- Text fields: 150-300px width
- Layout: Proper VBox/HBox alignment
